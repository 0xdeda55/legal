Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the application ("Service") collects, uses, and protects your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you connect third-party accounts (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram), we store OAuth tokens necessary to publish content on your behalf. We do not store your passwords.

Content Data

The Service stores content you create, including text, images, and videos, locally on your device or server. This data is used solely for the purpose of managing and publishing your content.

Usage Data

The Service may collect basic usage data such as content creation history and publishing activity for the purpose of improving the user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate with third-party platforms on your behalf
  • Create, manage, and publish content as directed by you
  • Improve the content creation workflow

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your own infrastructure. The Service does not transmit your data to any external servers other than the third-party platforms you explicitly connect to (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram) for the purpose of publishing content.

5. Third-Party Services

The Service integrates with the following third-party services:

  • YouTube (Google) — for video uploading and thumbnail management
  • TikTok — for video uploading
  • Instagram (Meta) — for Reels publishing
  • Google AI (Gemini) — for image generation
  • Anthropic (Claude) — for content generation

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide the Service (i.e., publishing to platforms you have authorized).

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained locally for as long as you use the Service. You may delete your data at any time by removing content from the application or deleting the local database.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. OAuth tokens are stored in a local database. We recommend securing access to your server and keeping your software up to date.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored by the Service
  • Delete any or all of your data
  • Disconnect third-party accounts at any time
  • Export your data

10. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

12. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
